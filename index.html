About DEBARAG DAS

View Profile Pic

DEBARAG DAS (Full Name: Deba Mohan Das) is a passionate independent singer, songwriter, and music composer from Majuli, Assam, a land known for its spiritual heritage and vibrant cultural traditions. Born on 31st December 2003, Debarag carries the soul of Assam into his music, crafting melodies that are heartfelt, emotive, and rooted in personal truth.

Specializing in original compositions, he creates songs that reflect real-life emotions, relationships, and stories—often blending poetic lyricism with expressive vocals. His songwriting captures both the simplicity and depth of human experience, making his work relatable across diverse audiences.

Debarag's artistic style draws from various influences, including Assamese folk, Hindi pop, and contemporary Indian melodies. His vocal delivery is sincere and soulful, often leaving a lasting impression on listeners. Each of his songs is a journey—carefully composed and passionately performed to convey meaning beyond just sound.

As an artist, he believes in the timeless power of music to heal, inspire, and connect. His vision is to bring fresh and meaningful music to the forefront of India's independent music scene, while staying true to his cultural roots and personal voice. With every release, Debarag aims to reach deeper into the hearts of listeners, crafting music that speaks not just to the ears, but to the soul.
