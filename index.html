About DEBARAG DAS

My Profile

DEBARAG DAS (Deba Mohan Das) is an independent singer, songwriter, and music composer from Majuli, Assam—a land of deep culture and spirituality. Born on 31st December 2003, he blends the soul of Assam into heartfelt, original songs.

His music tells real stories—about love, life, and emotions—through poetic lyrics and expressive vocals. Drawing from Assamese folk, Hindi pop, and modern melodies, Debarag creates songs that connect deeply with listeners.

Known for his soulful voice and honest songwriting, he believes music can heal, inspire, and bring people together. With every release, Debarag brings fresh, meaningful music that touches the heart and stays true to his roots.
